My Vision

As a Biomedical Engineer, I design technology not only for efficiency but for sustainability. By leveraging Deep Learning and Computer Vision, I analyze not just how fast a system performs, but how resilient its foundations truly are.

My expertise lies in cutting through the noise of complex data to uncover invisible risks regarding human health and system integrity. My goal is to architect solutions that deliver proactive foresight rather than reactive fixes.

I am driven by a core engineering philosophy: You should never wait for a crisis to strengthen a system; the best time to repair the roof is when the sun is shining.
